Step 1 of 6

Make sure you have the following tools that you will need to complete this task.
- A means of transportation.
- A beer opener may be needed. If you do not have one just buy cans instead of bottles.

Step 2 of 6

Go to the store and purchase a beer of your choice.

Step 3 of 6

Make sure beer is cold. If it isn’t, place in the fridge. Try again in 30 minutes.

Step 4 of 6

If your beer has come in a glass bottle, there are 2 ways to open. If the bottle is a twist off, just use ur hand to twist off the cap. If it is not, use a bottle opener to force the cap off. If the beer has come in a can then just open it using the tab on the top. (Bottle opener instructional app coming in 2016)

Step 5 of 6

Now that your beer has been opened, you can drink it from its container or pour into a glass. Now you can drink your beer!

Step 6 of 6

Repeat steps 3-5 as many times as you’d like or until you run out of beer. Have fun and drink responsibly. (or until you pass out)
